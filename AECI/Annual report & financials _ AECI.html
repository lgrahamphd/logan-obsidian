Annual report & financials

2023 annual report showcases Associated Electric Cooperative's "Power with Purpose"

Associated's 2023 annual report - “Power with Purpose” - highlights Associated's members-first mindset as a differentiating advantage over other power suppliers. While energy industry trends and external pressures point towards a potentially less reliable and more expensive future, the report outlines how Associated and its 700 employees are unwavering in our member-led mission.

Together, we call it Power with Purpose.
